Collect User Feedback with Non-Intrusive Slide-In Widgets

Traditional feedback forms often disrupt the user experience, leading to low response rates and skewed data. Omoik's slide-in feedback widgets offer a modern, user-friendly alternative that captures authentic insights without interrupting the user journey.

Our slide-in widgets appear at strategic moments in the user journey, triggered by specific actions, time spent on page, or scroll depth. This contextual approach ensures you're collecting feedback when it's most relevant, increasing both the quantity and quality of responses.

Key Benefits of Slide-In Feedback Widgets

  • Higher Response Rates: Non-intrusive design leads to 3-5x more responses compared to traditional pop-ups.
  • Contextual Insights: Gather feedback at specific points in the user journey for more actionable data.
  • Customizable Design: Match your brand's look and feel for a seamless experience.
  • Improved User Experience: Respect your visitors' browsing experience while still collecting valuable feedback.
